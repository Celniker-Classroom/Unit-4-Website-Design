Basic Rules and Guidelines

Rules:

  • Always pay for your visit
  • Keep a safe distance away from other climbers
  • Each climb has a specific color, only use that color for that specific climb
  • No running
  • Some gyms require all clothing to be on
  • Food and drink policies differ, check with your local gym

Etiquette:

  • Take climbing shoes off before entering bathrooms
  • Take turns
  • Notify staff of messes or accidents when you see them
  • Do not clap chalked hands together
  • Do not overuse chalk
  • Do not offer advice if they did not ask for it, they may want to figure it out for themselves

How to fall:

  1. Land on your feet
  2. Squat to absorb impact
  3. Roll onto your back

Break the rules to see the consequences!
